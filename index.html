🫙 Ghar ke Nuskhe — For Your Skin Type

💛
Haldi + Curd Face Pack

Mix ¼ tsp haldi with 1 tbsp thick dahi. Apply for 15 min. Fights bacteria, brightens spots, soothes redness. Use twice a week.

🌹
Gulab Jal Toner

Pure rose water as toner after cleansing. Balances pH, hydrates, and smells divine. Store in fridge for extra cooling effect.

🟤
Multani Mitti Mask

Mix with rose water only (not plain water). For oily T-zone, once a week. Draws out excess sebum without over-stripping.

🥒
Kheere ka Ras (Cucumber)

Slice fresh cucumber, refrigerate, press on cheeks and dark spots for 10 min. Natural lightening + intense cooling.

🔄 Gentle Changes We Suggest

🥗
Reduce dairy + processed sugar for 3 weeks

Your T-zone congestion pattern matches high-dairy diet indicators. Try switching to oat milk chai for 3 weeks and watch your cheek texture improve noticeably. Add sabza seeds (basil seeds) to your water for skin hydration.

🏃
Cleanse within 20 minutes post-workout

Sweat + sebum sitting on skin for 1+ hours is a primary cause of your enlarged pores. A gentle micellar wipe right after works if a full wash isn't possible.

🛏️
Switch to a silk or satin pillowcase

Cotton creates friction that aggravates skin texture. Silk keeps serums on your face instead of absorbing them. Change to silk and see difference in cheek lines within 2-3 weeks.

✨
Add Niacinamide 10% to your routine

For your specific pattern of enlarged pores + mild pigmentation + oily T-zone, Niacinamide is the one ingredient doing triple duty. Apply after toner, before moisturizer, in the morning.
